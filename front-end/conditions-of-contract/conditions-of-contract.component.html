CONDITIONS OF CONTRACT - INTERNATIONAL


Notice concerning carrier's limitation of liability


If the carriage involves an ultimate destination or stop in a country other than the country of departure, the Montreal Convention or the Warsaw Convention may be applicable to the liability of the Carrier in respect of loss of, damage or delay to cargo. Carrier's limitation of liability in accordance with those conventions shall be as set forth in sub-paragraph 4 unless a higher value is declared.
